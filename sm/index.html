Comparisons

We compare our method to two additional baselines:

  • Only-zoom. Following the online AI community, we assume the constant-depth scene, and remove the depth prediction model from the method.
  • 2D Splatting. We replace our unified 3D representation with 2D rendering, i.e., warping the content from the current frame to the next, using a depth-aware splatting warping [1]
